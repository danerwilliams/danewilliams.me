About Me

I am an undergraduate computer science student at the University of Notre Dame graduating in 2022. Outside of my studies I spend much of my time at the pool playing for the Notre Dame men’s water polo team. Originally, I am from the Bellevue/Eastside area of Seattle, where I attended Eastside Catholic School and graduated in 2018. I am also a proud alumni of the Bellevue Club Swim Team. Since leaving for college my family moved to the Bay Area so that is where I reside while on breaks. While at Notre Dame I live in Fisher Hall.
